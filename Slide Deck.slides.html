Ford GoBikes Analysis¶

by Srinivas Ayer¶

1. Introduction¶

Ford GoBike is a bike sharing company in San Fransisco that has provided data for its trips from 2017 to 2020.

Goal of the project

  1. Find the most common duration for bike rides.
  2. Analyze the data and gather insights about the growth in the ridership of the company since 2017.
  3. Identify the busiest stations that have maximum bike rides and number of bikes that need to be kept at a station to satisfy demand.
  4. Most popular ride days.
  5. Busiest time to ride a bike and if there is a relationship with the week day.

2. Data Overview¶

From the AWS server the data was programatically downloaded and unzipped.

28 different csv files were retrieved and merged to form a master dataset.

This dataset contains 6 million rows and 14 columns and is about 1.2Gb in size. This data was cleaned for analysis.

The key columns for interest for us are the duration, the start time and end time of the rides. The rides in each year, hour, weekday and the starting and ending stations are also useful for analysis.

4. Conclusion¶

The analysis has identified stations and their corresponding peak demand for bikes on an hourly basis. Moreover following conclusions were drawn from the presentation-

  1. The bike rides are very popular between 7am to 10am and 4pm to 7pm on weekdays.
  2. Weekdays have considerably more traffic than weekends.
  3. There seems to be a steady rise in ridership from 2017 to 2020.
  4. People mostly prefer short rides lasting between 5 min and 10 min on weekdays, but on an average 6 min longer rides on weekends.
